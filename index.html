Where It All Began!

My name is Matthias Maes, I'm an independent game production student at Digital Arts & Entertainment at Howest University of Applied Sciences in Belgium.
My journey of how I came to love programming started with Python automation, then evolved into web development with HTML, CSS, and a bit of JavaScript. Minecraft sparked my love for game creation, where I designed small games, mazes and dungeons, and made datapacks for extra fun.
That's how I got into game development. Now, I combine my creativity and programming skills to craft unique games. As a quick learner, problem-solver, and team player, I'm excited to grow and make my mark in the industry.
